Date and Time Functions

1. var=Date(Format) - To display current date in given Format


2. Date(Format,timespan) - To Convert given time in seconds converted into date format

timespan - total seconds from 01/01/1970 date


3. $seconds=strtotime("String Date/Format'); --It converts string date into a Total Seconds

Today
